About Our School

At Mainaga San Francisco Integrated School, we are dedicated to providing a nurturing and stimulating environment where every child can achieve their full potential. Our mission is to inspire a lifelong love of learning and foster intellectual, social, and emotional growth.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that empowers students to become compassionate, critical thinkers and responsible global citizens who are prepared to meet the challenges of the future.

Our Mission

We are committed to delivering a comprehensive and engaging curriculum, fostering a supportive community, and celebrating diversity. Our goal is to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary for success in all aspects of life.

History

Founded in 1985, Mainaga San Francisco Integrated School began as a small community school with a big vision. Over the decades, we have grown and evolved, but our core values remain the same: a commitment to academic excellence, student well-being, and community engagement. We are proud of our rich history and the thousands of students who have passed through our doors and gone on to make a positive impact on the world.
